How LIVE4 works
LIVE4 does live streaming from your GoPro to your favorite platforms: Facebook, YouTube, Twitter (Periscope), etc.
What I need to stream live with LIVE4
Your GoPro, your smartphone (iOS or Android), and cellular data turned on.
Connectivity
LIVE4 talks to your GoPro via Wi-Fi. To send video stream (to Facebook, for instance) LIVE4 uses cellular data.
Accounts and platforms
We have two options for live streaming: streaming to Facebook and streaming to multiple platforms (YouTube, Periscope, Facebook and more) simultaneously.
To stream to Facebook, you need to connect your Facebook account. Learn more.
To stream to multiple platforms, you need to connect your Restream account. Learn more
