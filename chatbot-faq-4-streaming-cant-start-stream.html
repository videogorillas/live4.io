Facebook permissions expired
Seems LIVE4 app does not have facebook permission to create live video.
Try to relogin via facebook. Open LIVE4, tap menu icon, logout from facebook and login via facebook again. Try to start streaming to your timeline, then try streaming to your page or group if you have it. Missing permissions should be asked.
Also may help deleting LIVE4 app from facebook settings: open facebook (web or app) -> settings -> Apps and web sites (or Business integrations) -> find LIVE4 app in the list and delete it. Open LIVE4 and relogin via facebook. Try to start streaming to your timeline, then try streaming to your page or group if you have it. Missing permissions should be asked.
No permissions to post to Facebook Page
You should have an “Andmin” or “Editor” role to stream to your page. You should have an “Admin” or “Moderator” role to stream to your Group.
