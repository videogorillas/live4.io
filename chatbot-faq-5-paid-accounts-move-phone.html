Transferring subscription to another phone
iOS
Use "Restore purchases" button in menu.
Android
  1. Open phone settings -> Apps -> LIVE4. Clear all app data and delete the app.
  2. Open Google Play Store.
  3. In the top of menu choose the account you used for buying the subscription.
  4. Install LIVE4 app again.
From iOS to Android or vice versa
Google Play and App Store are separate companies and each store has their own subscriptions. So you should buy another subscription for your new phone.
