How to make a perfect stream
1
Ensure you have a good mobile data connection. LIVE4 works best with LTE connection.
2
Check that LIVE4 app privacy isn't lower than your desired stream privacy. (More info here)
3
Ensure that no Facebook permissions had expired (if you hadn't used LIVE4 for some time).
4
Double-check all Restream channel settings.
5
If you stream an important event, make a small test stream beforehand.
6
Keep your GoPro close to your smartphone so that it doesn't loose Wi-Fi connection.
7
Make your GoPro's Wi-Fi network "Auto-Join" in your smartphone settings. This will help LIVE4 to reconnect to your GoPro in case it loses connection.
Remove "Auto-Join" option for all the other known Wi-Fi networks around.
8
Make sure your GoPro has enough space on SD card.
9
Periodically check that you GoPro is not overheated.
