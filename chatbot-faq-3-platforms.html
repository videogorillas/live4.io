Which platforms can I stream to?
With LIVE4, you can stream to Facebook, YouTube, Twitter (Periscope), Twitch, LinkedIn and more.

Facebook or Restream
We have two options for live streaming: streaming to Facebook and streaming to multiple platforms (YouTube, Periscope, Facebook and more) simultaneously.
To stream to Facebook, you need to connect your Facebook account. Learn more.
To stream to multiple platforms, you need to connect your Restream account. Learn more.

What is multistreaming?
Multistreaming is streaming to several platforms at once. When you connect your Restream account to LIVE4, you can select as many platforms as you want. Your single stream will be sent to those platforms simultaneously.

You can connect YouTube, Periscope or Twitch as a platform when you're multistreaming.
